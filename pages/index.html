About JobConnect

At JobConnect you will benefit from a stimulating career at an established, stable company with an exciting start-up vibe. Competitive salaries and generous benefits mean peace of mind, so you can get on with the task of continuing JobConnect.com’s reign as the creators of some of SA’s most popular websites. These include the inimitable News24, South Africa’s biggest online news source, and a range of specialised and niche sites such as Channel24, Wheels24 and Food24 (to name just a few!). As a JobConnect.com employee you’ll join the dynamic teams who source, create, and distribute all the media associated with these sites.

Our flat hierarchy means closer relationships between all levels of management, allowing for greater collaboration between teams and numerous opportunities to learn and grow. JobConnect.com adheres to a results-driven approach which creates an environment of innovation and collaboration, and assures our place at the forefront of the industry. Being a member of Media24 under the Naspers banner provides you with a range of exciting career pathways too.

A number of perks will keep you motivated and energised – from our CSI coffee initiative which provides employees with inexpensive quality coffee from our in-house barista (proceeds go to various charities) to our WPA registered running club, where employee memberships are subsidized and race entries fees are refunded. Based in the CBD, you’ll have the sights and sounds of the city at your feet, with the closest possible access to all major transport routes and facilities.
